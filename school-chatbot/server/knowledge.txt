Takshila Educational Society laid the foundation of veritable learning in Patna by establishing Delhi Public School in the year 1998 in a collaborative effort with the Delhi Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. DPS Patna is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers schooling from Pre-Nursery to Class XII. The last 27 years have been a journey where perseverance and progress have been the cornerstones. Today, it can proudly claim to be one of the most sought after schools in Eastern India.

The school is a marvel of modern architecture with well-lit, airy classrooms, an outdoor theatre, performing art studios, fully equipped science laboratories, a cafeteria, spacious courtyards for indoor games, and beautifully landscaped lawns - all of which offer a vibrant and multidisciplinary learning space. It is dedicated to nurturing future leaders who succeed globally while staying rooted in Indian values. The school focuses on the holistic development of each student, promoting cognitive, emotional, physical, and aesthetic growth through a wide range of curricular and co-curricular activities. With a strong emphasis on life skills, values, environmental awareness and sports, DPS Patna offers a balanced, value-driven, and stress-free learning experience.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMME
Academics at DPS Patna go beyond textbooks. We encourage students to explore co-curricular and extracurricular activities that enhance their knowledge and skills in various domains, including sports, arts, music, and leadership programs.

Curriculum
We follow a comprehensive and structured curriculum that integrates academic excellence with real-world applications. Our curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, ensuring a balanced education that prepares students for higher studies and help keep pace with the evolving world.

Grade Levels & Subjects

Junior Wing Academic Programme (JWAP):

Classes Pre-Nursery to III: A foundation in literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts.
Common Academic Programme (CAP):
Classes IV-X: Strengthening core subjects (English, Math, Science, Social Science, Hindi, Sanskrit, ICT) with interdisciplinary learning.
Classes XI-XII: Advanced coursework with subject specializations and career readiness.
Teaching Methodology.

Teaching Methodology
Our innovative teaching approaches include:

Experiential Learning: Hands-on activities, projects, and field trips.
Technology Integration: Smart classrooms, digital resources, and e-learning platforms.
Collaborative Learning: Group discussions, teamwork, and peer learning
Student-Centric Approach: Individual attention and personalized learning strategies.
Assessment & Evaluation

Classes Pre-Nursery to III: The academic year is divided into two terms. There are no formal examinations and assessments are done on the basis of day-to-day observations under Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation.

Classes IV-XII: The academic year is divided into two terms. A continuous and comprehensive evaluation system is followed which includes:

Formative Assessments: Quizzes, assignments, and interactive sessions.
Summative Assessments: Mid-term and final exams (IV-IX & XI)/ Board Exams (X & XII) to assess overall understanding.
Skill-Based Evaluations: Practical exams, presentations, and creative projects.
